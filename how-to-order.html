Our Process & How to Order

Step 1: Consultation & Quote

It all starts with your idea. Reach out to us with what you're looking for, the quantity you need, and any artwork you may have. We'll discuss your options and provide a detailed quote.

Step 2: Design & Mockup

We'll work with your design or help create a new one. For embroidery, we'll digitize your art to prepare it for stitching. Before we produce anything, you will receive a digital mockup for your final approval.

Step 3: Production

Once you approve the mockup, we get to work! Using our commercial-grade equipment, we'll create your custom items with precision and care, ensuring a professional, high-quality result.

Step 4: Completion & Pickup

We'll notify you as soon as your order is ready. You can then stop by our shop to pick up your custom creations.
