Custom Embroidery

At The Strong Stitch, we take pride in offering small-business service with big-business quality. Specializing in custom embroidery, we deliver exceptional craftsmanship on a wide range of fabric items, from clothing and hats to bags and more. Whether you’re personalizing for yourself, your business, or your school, we make sure your designs come to life with precision and professionalism.

Our commitment to high-quality results is backed by our use of top-of-the-line, commercial-grade equipment. We utilize 5 Happy Japan embroidery machines — one with 12 needles and four with 15 needles — capable of handling the most intricate designs with remarkable speed and accuracy. These machines, combined with our skilled artisans, ensure that every project meets the highest standards of durability and detail.

Whether it’s a custom business logo, a school emblem, or even a simple number on the side of a hat, The Strong Stitch delivers professional-grade results that stand up to the demands of everyday use.

With us, you get personalized service and attention, backed by the quality and capabilities of a large-scale operation. Let The Strong Stitch help you turn your ideas into high-quality embroidered products!
