About the Websters

Amanda Webster (Cokee)

Amanda Webster (Cokee) was born in Ft Lauderdale, Florida and shortly after relocated to South Lakeland. Amanda is well known in the South Lakeland area for her records in competitive swimming. After graduating from George Jenkins High School in 2006, she attended Florida Atlantic University and Florida Southern College and swam competitively for both schools. After graduating from Polk State College, she began her career as a Physical Therapy Assistant. Several years later, she decided to be a stay-at-home mom to spend time with her 3 kids; Jayce, Alivia, and Reese.

Jordan Webster

Jordan Webster was born and raised in South Lakeland and grew up playing baseball in Highland City. Shortly after graduating from George Jenkins High School in 2005 he became a firefighter and is currently a Captain/Paramedic for Polk County Fire Rescue. Jordan continued his education to include a degree in Fire Science and Emergency Medical Services. Jordan has several years of Graphic Design underneath his belt as well. He hopes to bring a more manly side to The Strong Stitch for all the guys needing some customization!

What makes us different?

98% of our products are made in-house from locally sourced suppliers. Communication and Customer Service play a vital role in our business. All of our machines are commercial grade quality. We're not a "Mom with a Cricut" business.
